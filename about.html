About KCJC

Developing young leaders today for a stronger Kwekwe tomorrow.

Who We Are

The Kwekwe City Junior Council (KCJC) is the official youth leadership body that represents children and young people within the City of Kwekwe. Working alongside Kwekwe City Council, schools and community partners, the Junior Council provides a platform where young people can participate in leadership, civic engagement and community development.

KCJC encourages young people to become responsible leaders by promoting integrity, teamwork, innovation, volunteerism and active citizenship. Members work together to identify issues affecting young people and contribute ideas and solutions that improve their schools and communities.

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To empower young people through leadership development, community service, advocacy and meaningful participation in local governance.

Our Vision

A generation of confident, responsible and visionary young leaders creating positive change throughout Kwekwe and Zimbabwe.

Our Values

Integrity • Respect • Accountability • Teamwork • Service • Inclusivity • Excellence

What We Do

  • Represent the voices of children and young people.
  • Promote children's rights and youth participation.
  • Organise community clean-up campaigns.
  • Lead environmental conservation initiatives.
  • Promote leadership development in schools.
  • Partner with Kwekwe City Council on youth programmes.
  • Encourage volunteerism and community service.
  • Support educational and awareness campaigns.
