About the Kwekwe City Junior Council

The Kwekwe City Junior Council (KCJC) serves as the official youth leadership body representing the young people of the City of Kwekwe. Working in partnership with Kwekwe City Council, schools and community organisations, the Junior Council provides young people with opportunities to participate in leadership, civic engagement and community development.

KCJC promotes responsible leadership, children's rights, environmental conservation, community service, education and youth empowerment. Through meetings, outreach programmes, clean-up campaigns, leadership conferences and public awareness initiatives, the Council equips young leaders with the skills required to become responsible citizens and future leaders of Zimbabwe.

Every council term brings together dedicated young leaders who represent the voices of children and young people while working to improve their schools and communities. Their commitment, innovation and passion continue to strengthen the legacy of the Kwekwe City Junior Council.
